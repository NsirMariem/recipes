Ingredients:
 - 1 cups sugar
 - 2 cups all-purpose flour
 - 1 cup cocoa
 - 1 teaspoons baking powder
 - 1 teaspoons baking soda
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 4 eggs
 - 1/2 cup milk
 - 1/2 cup vegetable oil
 - 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
 - 400g dark chocolat

Directions:
 1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees C. Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking
    pans.
 
 2. In a large bowl, combine the sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda
    and salt.  Add eggs, milk, oil, vanilla and chocolat. Mix until smooth. scoop it.
    
 3. Bake 30 to 35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out
    clean. Cool completely.
